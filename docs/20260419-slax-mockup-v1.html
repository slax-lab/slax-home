Why Slax

A few ways we're different.

We don't chase the AI-reads-for-you race. Reading is the thing. AI should make reading easier, not replace it.

We don't build feeds or streaks. No dopamine games. The product should be usable — and closeable — without pulling at you.

We don't sell your content. Your library is yours. Export, delete, leave — your data comes with you.
