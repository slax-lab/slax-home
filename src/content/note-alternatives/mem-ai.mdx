## What Mem AI Does Well

Let's be honest. Mem has built one of the most polished AI-notes experiences out there, and Mem 2.0 (early 2026) made a real jump.

What stands out:

- **Mem Chat across your full archive** — ask "what did I write about X last quarter" and get a real answer
- **Smart Tags + Related Notes + Heads Up** — old notes surface in new ones automatically. The "second brain" promise actually delivered
- **Meeting Briefings (beta, Pro only)** — pulls relevant notes/context for upcoming calendar events. Heads Up Live extends this in real time during meetings
- **Voice Mode** — audio + transcript saved together, brain dumps turn into organized notes. Voice is a first-class input, not an afterthought
- **Templates + Collections** — structure on demand without rigid hierarchy
- **Calendar sync (Google/Outlook), Claude Connector, referral program** — recent shipping cadence is high
- **Desktop on Mac and Windows** — full clients on both. Slax Note doesn't have desktop

If your daily problem is "I want my notes to work *for* me," Mem is one of the few products that delivers on it.

## Where Slax Note Goes Different

The short version: Mem optimizes for *retrieval and resurfacing*. Slax Note optimizes for *output shapes from today's recording*.

### One recording, many shapes (Prompt-based)

Mem has Templates — reusable structures for meetings, interviews, project plans. You drop content into the template's shape.

Slax Note's Prompts are different. You speak for two minutes, then pick a Prompt:

- *"Turn this into a 280-character tweet, sharp opener."*
- *"Make this a LinkedIn post, 200 words, professional tone."*
- *"Rewrite as a blog draft with subheads."*
- *"Pull out the decisions into a bullet list."*

Same audio, four outputs, side by side. You keep what you want. Prompts are user-defined and shareable — write your own, share with friends.

If you publish to more than one channel, this is the day-to-day difference.

### Android exists

Mem doesn't ship Android. If your phone is Android, Slax Note is a real option and Mem isn't.

### Pricing

Mem Pro is $12/mo. Slax Note Pro is $4.99/mo or $49.99/yr. Roughly a third the cost. You're paying for different products, so price alone isn't the right frame — but it's not nothing.

## How we think about this

Two beliefs shape Slax Note.

**Personal first, not meetings.** Meeting tools pull a product toward speaker separation, calendar integration, attendee detection, enterprise pricing — each one drags focus away from the person walking down the street with an idea. We picked a side. Slax Note is for the person, not the meeting room. (Mem's recent Meeting Briefings beta is a sign they're moving the other direction; that's a fine choice, just not ours.)

**Built to be here in five years, growing with you.** The point of voice notes isn't today's recording. It's what your library looks like after three years — the walk-the-dog idea from last March that helps you write something today, the half-thought from a year ago that turns out to be the seed of next month's project. Mem optimizes for retrieving from that library. We optimize for keeping the product simple enough that you keep using it for the next three years in the first place. Different bets on the same long-term value.

## What Slax Note Doesn't Have Yet

We'd rather you switch with both eyes open. What Mem AI does today that we don't:

- **Strong AI chat across your full archive** — our search is basic. If long-term recall is your main job, Mem is currently better
- **Smart Tags, Related Notes, Heads Up** — we don't surface related old notes when you write new ones
- **Meeting Briefings + calendar sync** — Mem pulls context from your calendar; we don't touch calendars
- **Mac and Windows desktop** — we have iOS, Android, and the web. No desktop client
- **Claude Connector** — your notes can't be piped into Claude as context from us today
- **Templates with structured fields** — we're free-form Prompts, not Mem-style structured templates
- **Long recordings** — we cap each recording at 30 minutes

If your daily reliance is on those, stay with Mem until we close any gaps that matter to you.
