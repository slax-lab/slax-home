Slax Reader is a read-later app built around actually finishing what you save — quick & nice parsing, permanent archives, AI outlines that help you move through long articles, and auto-tagging that handles the organizing so you don't have to.

This is where we write about what we're building and why.

- Product updates when something significant ships.
- Occasionally, how we think about reading and the tools people use for it.

Not a newsletter, just things worth saying when there's something worth saying.
