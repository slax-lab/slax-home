The browser extension is the fastest way to save articles while you browse.

## Install

1. Go to the [Chrome Extension page](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/gdnhaajlomjkhahnmiijphnodkcfikfd)
2. Click **Add to Chrome**
3. Pin the extension to your toolbar

## Save an Article

Click the Slax Reader icon while on any page — the article is saved and archived instantly.
