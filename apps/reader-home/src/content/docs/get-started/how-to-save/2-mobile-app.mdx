---
title: Save with Mobile App
description: Use the iOS or Android share sheet to save articles to Slax Reader from any app.
---

Save articles directly from Safari, Chrome, Twitter, or any app that supports the share sheet.

## iOS

1. Open an article in any app
2. Tap the **Share** button
3. Select **Slax Reader** from the list

## Android

1. Open an article in any app
2. Tap **Share**
3. Select **Slax Reader**
