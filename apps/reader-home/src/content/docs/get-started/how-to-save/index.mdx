Start saving articles to your permanent library in seconds. Pick the method that fits your workflow best.

## Save Methods

- **[1. Browser Extension](/reader/get-started/how-to-save/1-browser-extension)** — One click to save any web page while browsing on your computer.
- **[2. Mobile App](/reader/get-started/how-to-save/2-mobile-app)** — Use the iOS or Android share sheet to save articles on the go.

---

Every article you save is permanently archived — no more broken links.
